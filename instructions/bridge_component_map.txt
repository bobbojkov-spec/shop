BRIDGE513 – HOME PAGE: REACT COMPONENT MAP
=========================================

App
└── BridgeLayout
    ├── <Header />
    ├── <MainContent />
    └── <Footer />

Header
------
<Header>
  ├── <TopBar /> (hidden/unused on this demo – reserved for announcements)
  └── <HeaderMain>
        ├── <Logo />
        ├── <PrimaryNav />
        ├── <HeaderActions />
        └── <MobileMenuToggle />

<Logo>
  - Displays brand mark “Bridge”
  - Links to "/"

<PrimaryNav>
  - Items:
    - Home
    - About Us
    - Our Stores
    - Shop
    - Blog
  - Desktop: horizontal list
  - Mobile: collapsible vertical list inside <MobileNavDrawer />

<HeaderActions>
  - <SearchToggle />
  - <CartPreview />
    - Empty state text: “No products in the cart.”
    - Mini-cart dropdown with product list when items present

<MobileNavDrawer>
  - Controlled by <MobileMenuToggle />
  - Contains duplicated <Logo>, navigation links, and possibly search/cart icons


MainContent
-----------
<MainContent>
  ├── <HeroFeatureStrip />
  ├── <ProductGridSection />
  ├── <DesignersHighlight />
  ├── <BlogPreviewSection />
  ├── <WorkshopPromoSection />
  ├── <GalleryStrip />
  ├── <FooterWidgetArea /> (conceptually part of footer, but structurally at bottom of page)
  └── (potential: <NewsletterBar /> / promotional blocks)

HeroFeatureStrip
----------------
<HeroFeatureStrip>
  - Three “card” style feature blocks in a row (desktop)
  - Stacked vertically on mobile

  <HeroFeatureCard />
    props:
      - title: string
      - subtitle: string (short descriptive line)
      - linkLabel: "learn more"
      - linkHref: URL (currently /shop or relevant internal links)
      - image: hero illustration/ceramics

    instances:
      1. "INSPIRED BY THE SEA"
      2. "CREATED WITH LOVE"
      3. "TOUCHED BY THE NATURE"


ProductGridSection
------------------
<ProductGridSection>
  - Title is visually implicit (“Best Sellers” / product listing feel)
  - Contains a grid of WooCommerce products

  structure:
    <SectionHeader>
      - Optional kicker text
      - Optional title (currently not explicitly rendered as heading text in snippet)

    <ProductGrid>
      - Responsive layout:
        - Desktop: 3–4 columns
        - Tablet: 2–3 columns
        - Mobile: 1–2 columns

      <ProductCard />
        props:
          - imageSrc
          - title
          - price
          - productUrl
          - isOnSale? (not visible here but theme supports)
          - wishlistEnabled? (true)
        children:
          - <ProductImage />
          - <WishlistButton label="Add to wishlist" />
          - <ProductTitle /> (e.g., "Rose Oval Plate")
          - <ProductPrice /> (e.g., "$300.00")
          - <AddToCartButton label="Add to cart" />

    <SectionFooter>
      - <ViewAllButton label="VIEW ALL" href="/shop" />


DesignersHighlight
------------------
<DesignersHighlight>
  - Text-led introduction to designers “EVA & NINA”
  - One-column text block with supporting visual

  structure:
    <SectionHeader>
      - <SectionKicker /> (optional)
      - <SectionTitle> "MEET DESIGNERS EVA & NINA"
      - <SectionSubtitle> (short lorem ipsum paragraph)

    <SectionBody>
      - <ReadMoreButton label="READ MORE" href="/about-us" />
      - <DesignersImage /> (hero or portrait, depending on layout)


BlogPreviewSection
------------------
<BlogPreviewSection>
  - 3-column blog teaser layout

  structure:
    <SectionHeader>
      - <SectionTitle> (implicit/hidden, e.g., “From Our Blog”)

    <BlogCardGrid>
      <BlogCard />
        props:
          - thumbnailImageSrc
          - title
          - authorName
          - date
          - href

        children (example data):
          - thumbnail: "Image: g", "Image: h", etc.
          - title:
              1. "Japan Design 2023: Handmade Ceramic Ideas"
              2. "London Design 2023: Make Unique Handmade Mugs"
              3. "Japan Design 2023: Color Inspo For All Visual Arts"
          - author: "Nina Marling"
          - date: 14–15 December, 2023


WorkshopPromoSection
--------------------
<WorkshopPromoSection>
  - Text + CTA section encouraging sign-ups for studio workshops

  structure:
    <SectionHeader>
      - <SectionTitle> "WORKSHOP IN OUR CERAMIC STUDIO"
      - <SectionSubtitle> short lorem ipsum paragraph

    <SectionBody>
      - <PrimaryButton label="APPLY NOW" href="/contact" or dedicated workshop page />
      - <WorkshopImageStrip />
          - 3–4 small decorative images (studio/people/ceramics)


GalleryStrip (could also be InstagramFeedSection)
-------------------------------------------------
<GalleryStrip>
  - 3–4 (or more) images in a horizontal, masonry-style or equal-grid layout

  <GalleryItem />
    props:
      - imageSrc
      - href (if using as Instagram link or lightbox)
      - overlayEnabled?

    features:
      - Hover overlay icon (e.g., view or Instagram icon)
      - Responsive wrapping on small screens


FooterWidgetArea + Footer
-------------------------
<Footer>
  ├── <FooterWidgets />
  └── <FooterBottom />

<FooterWidgets>
  - Multi-column widget area

  columns (approx as per content):
    1. <FooterWidgetSearch />
       - Heading: "SEARCH"
       - <SearchField />

    2. <FooterWidgetContact />
       - Heading: "contact"
       - Phone number: "+1123456789"
       - Address link to Google Maps

    3. <FooterWidgetLinks title="Help" />
       - Links: FAQ’s, Pricing Plans, Track Your Order, Returns

    4. <FooterWidgetLinks title="About Us" />
       - Links: Our Story, Job Opportunities, Store Locator, Contact Us

    5. <FooterWidgetLinks title="Customer Services" />
       - Links: My Account, Terms of Use, Deliveries & Returns, Gift card

<FooterBottom>
  - <FooterCopyright>
      text: "© 2024 QODE INTERACTIVE, ALL RIGHTS RESERVED"
      link: qodeinteractive.com
  - Optional: <BackToTopButton />


Cross-cutting Components & Utilities
------------------------------------
- <Section>
    props:
      - id
      - backgroundColor
      - paddingY
      - maxWidth
      - align (left/center)
      - hasContainer (true/false)

- <Container>
    - Handles max-width and horizontal padding

- <Grid>
    props:
      - columnsDesktop
      - columnsTablet
      - columnsMobile
      - gap

- <Button variant="primary" | "ghost" | "link" />

- <Typography>
    - <Heading level={1|2|3|4|5|6} />
    - <BodyText size="sm|md|lg" />

- <Image>
    props:
      - src
      - alt
      - loading
      - aspectRatio

- <Overlay>
    props:
      - opacity
      - backgroundColor
      - visibleOnHover
